    AMROD’S COMMITMENT

    Through the continuous pursuit of excellence and innovation, we aim to provide our clients with a seamless Total-Solution. Everything we do is geared towards managing complexity and making business convenient, simple and cost-effective, so our clients can focus on what they do best, SELLING!

    Our mission is to leave lasting impressions. It’s why we are obsessed with how we brand our products and how they make a real mark in the minds of those who receive them. More importantly, we aim to leave an impression on our customers and their customers through our Total-Solution.

    We are proud to be the largest supplier of branded promotional products in the southern hemisphere, supplying over 6 500 resellers through our network of 11 showrooms, across six African countries. Take a virtual tour of our Johannesburg showroom to get a feel for what we offer. We service 14 markets across the African continent and support all of these activities from our industry-leading 38 000m2 facility in Johannesburg. Take a look at our company profile to learn more about us.

    We search the world to source the most extensive range of unique products to provide our customers with exceptional choice. Our deep inventory, state-of-the-art branding facility and warehouse, ensures that we continuously deliver on our promise. We consistently offer and maintain 24 - 48hr lead-times on branded orders. Our innovative systems and experienced team, manages the complexity associated with branding over 250 000 products daily, across 2 500 unique orders - that’s over 75 million branded products every year.
    Watch this video to get a glimpse of what we are all about.

    Everything we do at Amrod is focused on creating a world-class experience and providing a seamless Total-Solution our customers can count on.

    Behind the scenes, Amrod is a team of 1 300 dedicated and passionate employees, who work tirelessly to deliver on our Total-Solution.
    Watch this video to meet the team and gain insight into our culture.

    OUR BRANDS

    BRANDS ARE POWERFUL! BRANDS ARE INSTANTLY RECOGNISABLE! BRANDS CREATE A CONNECTION!

    A great brand is more than a label or a logo. It is an idea that inspires confidence and trust in consumers, as well as a distinct sense of style, sophistication and craftsmanship.

    APPAREL BRANDS

    Our industry-leading apparel brands are designed to fit your work and lifestyle. Combining contemporary styles, premium quality construction and universal appeal, these brands are designed to make an impact.

    GIFTING BRANDS

    Our exceptional range of gifting brands provide you with the opportunity to choose from a premium selection of beautifully packaged and unique items, which aim to please. Our brands continue to grow in popularity and have become an exciting part of our offering.

    CUSTOM PRODUCTS

    Choose from a selection of customisable products including coolers, drawstring bags, totes, shoppers, gift boxes and more! This offering provides infinite gifting and packaging possibilities for brands that want to connect with their customers from start to finish.

    DISPLAY

    Our display solutions provide the perfect platform to advertise your brand anywhere you choose, allowing you to generate brand awareness and market your products or services to a wide audience with ease. Choose from over 200 indoor and outdoor display solutions to suit any environment or requirement.
